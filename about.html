Welcome to Our Mango

Aamrai is a beautiful mango nagpur where different varieties of mangoes are grown with care. Mango is known as the "King of Fruits" because of its sweet taste, rich flavor, and nutritional value. Our orchard provides fresh, juicy, and high-quality mangoes every season.

Mission

Our mission is to provide naturally grown mangoes to customers while maintaining a healthy and eco-friendly environment. We believe in quality farming and customer satisfaction.

Types of Mangoes Available

  • Alphonso (Hapus)
  • Kesar
  • Langra
  • Totapuri
  • Dasheri
  • Badami
  • Chausa
  • Neelam
  • Rajapuri
  • Pairi

Benefits of Mangoes

  1. Rich in Vitamins A and C.
  2. Boosts immunity.
  3. Good source of fiber.
  4. Helps in digestion.
  5. Provides natural energy.
